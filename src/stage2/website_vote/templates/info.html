Information & Consent

Welcome! Your participation in this study is highly appreciated. This voting interface is a key component of a Master Thesis research project aimed at evaluating and comparing the outputs of different AI models.

About the Task:

  • You will be presented with pairs of text-based outputs. These outputs are generated by AI models for specific tasks related to software development contracts.
  • Your role is to carefully review both outputs in each pair.
  • Based on your judgment, you will select the output that you consider to be of higher quality, more accurate, more helpful, or generally better according to the implicit task.
  • After submitting your choice for a pair, you will be automatically presented with the next available comparison, if any.

Instructions:

Please scan through the contract (will open in a new tab) and read both reports carefully and choose the one you believe is:

  • Clear and coherent: information is well-organized.
  • Accurate: correctly references the clauses.
  • Actionable: contains specific, practical recommendations.

Select the version that you, as a legal reviewer, deem most useful.

All data collected is anonymous and will be used solely for academic research purposes. Your thoughtful evaluations are crucial for the success of this project. You can do as many comparisons as you want, and you can stop at any time.

Thank you for your time and contribution!
